# LDOA Example Flow — Short Version v1.0
## From Human Declaration to Operational Continuity

## 1. Purpose

This document presents a short example of **Language-Driven Ontological Architecture (LDOA)**.

The example uses a small business situation:

> A seller says through WhatsApp:  
> “I sold 3 kilograms of potatoes.”

The purpose of this document is to show the basic flow of LDOA, not to describe the internal implementation of a specific runtime.

BODY may be understood as one possible runtime implementation of this pattern, but the focus here is LDOA itself.

This example follows the core definition of LDOA:

> An architectural pattern in which situations from reality are attended by Beings operating within the ontological domain of language, maintaining operational continuity.

---

## 2. Simplified Flow

For this example, the flow is grouped into three simplified areas:

```text
Human
→ System
→ Operation / Business
```

The public flow is:

```text
Human Declaration
→ System Mediation
→ Structured Situation
→ Operation / Business Contextualization
→ Being Attention
→ Declaration, Affirmation, or Breakdown
→ Operational Continuity
```

This is not:

```text
message → process → register → notify
```

It is:

```text
situation → attention → Speech Act → new attendable situation → continuity
```

---

## 3. The Example

A customer buys 3 kilograms of potatoes.

The human operator performs the sale and declares it through WhatsApp:

```text
I sold 3 kilograms of potatoes.
```

That declaration expresses a situation from reality.

The system does not create that reality.

The system makes the declaration treatable and routable.

For example, it may classify the declaration as:

```text
verb: sell
action_type: register
action_object: sale
attributes:
  product: potatoes
  quantity: 3
  unit: kilograms
```

This structured situation is not the final business result.

It is an attendable situation.

In the Operation / Business area, the structured situation becomes available for Beings whose promises include attending it.

A Sales Being may attend it if it falls within its promise.

For example:

```text
Attend structured situations where:
action_type = register
action_object = sale
```

The Sales Being contextualizes the situation using the relevant business and domain context.

That context may include:

```text
business
store
operator
rules
catalog
inventory
authority
sales conditions
registration rules
```

If the Sales Being fulfills its promise and has authority, it may produce a Declaration:

```text
Declaration:
The sale was registered / accepted in the Sales domain.
```

That Declaration changes the world of the Sales domain.

Other Beings may attend that Declaration only if it falls within their own promise.

If the Sales Being needs to confirm or report the result without transforming the domain again, it may produce an Affirmation:

```text
Affirmation:
The sale registration was completed.
```

If the Sales Being cannot fulfill its promise, it produces a Breakdown:

```text
Breakdown:
The sale could not be registered because the product was not recognized.
```

That Breakdown becomes a new Situation to be attended.

---

## 4. Core Rules Applied in the Example

```text
The system makes the human declaration treatable and routable.

Interpretation extracts and classifies the actionable structure.

Operation / Business makes the structured situation available for attention.

Operation / Business contextualizes the structured situation through Beings.

Domains do not attend situations; Beings do.

A Being attends only if the situation falls within its promise.

A Being attends a Situation only if it falls within its promise.

A Being produces a Speech Act after attending.

Other Beings attend produced Speech Acts only if they fall within their promise.

Promise determines what a Being may attend.

Authority determines the operational force of what a Being may produce.

A Declaration transforms operational reality when authority exists.

An Affirmation confirms, reports, coordinates, or leaves trace without transforming that reality by itself.

A Breakdown exposes that a Being could not continue or fulfill its promise, and becomes a new Situation.

Operational continuity is maintained through Situations being attended and Speech Acts being produced.
```

---

## 5. Summary

In short:

```text
Reality is expressed as a human declaration.
The system makes the declaration treatable and routable.
The result is a structured situation.
Operation / Business makes that structured situation available for attention.
A Being attends it only if it falls within its promise.
The Being contextualizes it and fulfills or attempts to fulfill its promise.
The Being produces a Declaration, Affirmation, or Breakdown.
Other Beings attend the produced Speech Act only if it falls within their promise.
Operational continuity is maintained.
```
